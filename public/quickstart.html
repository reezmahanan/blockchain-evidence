Quick Start Guide

Get up and running in minutes

üöÄ Welcome to EVID-DGC!

EVID-DGC is a blockchain-based evidence management system that ensures secure, transparent, and immutable handling of digital evidence for law enforcement and legal professionals.

Login Options

You have two ways to access the system:

1
MetaMask Wallet (Recommended)

Connect your wallet, select your role, and complete registration for blockchain authentication.

2
Email & Password

Use traditional email login for quick access without blockchain integration.

Getting Started with MetaMask

1
Install MetaMask

Download the MetaMask browser extension from metamask.io

2
Connect Your Wallet

Click "Connect MetaMask" on the homepage and approve the connection request.

3
Select Your Role

Choose from 8 specialized roles: Public Viewer, Investigator, Forensic Analyst, Legal Professional, Court Official, Evidence Manager, Auditor, or Administrator.

4
Complete Registration

Fill in your details (name, badge number, department) and submit to access your dashboard.

üí° Tip: Make sure you're connected to the Polygon network for the best experience.

Common Tasks

üì§ Uploading Evidence (Investigators/Analysts)

  1. Navigate to the evidence upload section
  2. Select an existing case or create a new one
  3. Choose your file (max 100MB)
  4. Add description and metadata
  5. Submit and verify upload success

üìÅ Creating a Case

  1. Click "Create New Case" from your dashboard
  2. Fill in case details and assign priority
  3. Add team members as needed
  4. Save and start adding evidence

üì• Downloading Evidence

  1. Navigate to the case evidence section
  2. Select the evidence item you need
  3. Click download (watermarked version will be provided)
  4. Verify download integrity
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Downloaded evidence is watermarked for chain of custody tracking.

Supported File Types

  • Images: JPEG, PNG, GIF
  • Videos: MP4, AVI, MOV
  • Audio: MP3, WAV, M4A
  • Documents: PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT
  • Archives: ZIP, RAR

Security Best Practices

  • Never share your wallet private keys
  • Use strong passwords for email accounts
  • Always log out when finished
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately
  • Keep MetaMask updated to the latest version
