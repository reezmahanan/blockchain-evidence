Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What is EVID-DGC?

EVID-DGC is a blockchain-based evidence management system that ensures secure, transparent, and immutable handling of digital evidence for law enforcement and legal professionals.

How secure is the system?

The system uses blockchain technology to create an immutable audit trail, ensuring that evidence cannot be tampered with once uploaded. All data is encrypted and access is controlled through role-based permissions.

What file types are supported?

The system supports various file types including images (JPEG, PNG, GIF), videos (MP4, AVI, MOV), audio files (MP3, WAV), documents (PDF, DOC, DOCX), and compressed files (ZIP, RAR).

Do I need MetaMask to use the system?

While MetaMask provides the most secure blockchain authentication, the system also supports traditional email/password login for users who prefer conventional authentication methods.

What blockchain network does EVID-DGC use?

EVID-DGC operates on the Polygon network, which provides fast, low-cost transactions while maintaining the security and immutability of blockchain technology. This ensures efficient evidence processing without high gas fees.

How long is evidence stored in the system?

Evidence is stored permanently on the blockchain and IPFS network, ensuring long-term preservation. The system follows legal retention requirements, with configurable retention policies based on case type and jurisdiction requirements.

Can evidence be deleted or modified after upload?

No, once evidence is uploaded and verified, it becomes immutable on the blockchain. This ensures the integrity of the chain of custody. Only authorized personnel can add annotations or status updates, which are recorded as separate blockchain transactions.

What compliance standards does EVID-DGC follow (ISO, NIST)?

EVID-DGC follows ISO 27001 for information security management, NIST Cybersecurity Framework guidelines, and adheres to digital forensics standards including ISO/IEC 27037 for digital evidence handling and chain of custody requirements.

How is my wallet key secured and protected?

Your private keys remain in your MetaMask wallet and are never transmitted to our servers. The system only uses your wallet address for identification and digital signatures. We implement zero-knowledge authentication to protect your privacy.

Can multiple users work on the same case simultaneously?

Yes, the system supports collaborative case management with real-time updates. Multiple authorized users can work on the same case simultaneously, with role-based permissions ensuring appropriate access levels and automatic conflict resolution.
